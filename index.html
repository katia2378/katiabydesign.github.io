ABOUT

Always passionate about design, I began my career in a sample room for home fashion brand Trendex. Among other duties, I worked on designing storyboards for upcoming trends in home fashion. Before long I was using Photoshop to create a digital storyboard and modifying images, which in turn inspired me to leave Trendex and enroll at RTC in Graphic Design. During my studies there I became very interested in Web Design and Development and begun learning HTML and CSS.
After completing a year and a half program I got my first graphics job in a small print shop. While working there I took some HTML+CSS courses at Dawson and soon enough I was freelancing. With more knowledge and experience I accepted a job at SCP SCIENCE where I worked both in print and web. During my time at SCP I began working towards a diploma in Web Development at Concordia.
Because I've always loved fashion I migrated to APP Group, a company that houses Mackage and Soia & Kyo fashion brands. I began as a Web Designer for Mackage, and currently hold a title of UX designer & Web Integrator for both brands.
