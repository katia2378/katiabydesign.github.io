Store Locator

Client

mackage.com, an e-commerce website for luxury fashion brand

The Challenge

Created a clear and concise way for customers to find Mackage, retailer, and online stores

My role

research, user flow, wireframing, prototyping

Problem Statement

With more Mackage stores opening each season, the need for a better way for customers to find the closest one was becoming very important. A problem also arose when several clients were looking online for deals and bought knockoff goods. After complaints to customer service, online stores section was implemented to give customers the safety of buying an authentic product from other retailers.
