Checkout Cart Desktop

Client

mackage.com, an e-commerce website for luxury fashion brand

The Challenge

Updated the checkout process to a be faster, easier and more efficient by creating a 2 STEP checkout.

My role

research, user flows, wireframing, visual design, prototyping

Problem Statement

A large drop off rate in different steps of the checkout process. Users were confused about whether the item was actually purchased. The misleading plus icons gave the impression of expandable menus. Confusion od country currency duties applicable
